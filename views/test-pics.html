How to buy a stock on Tajiri

Success

Minting a new stock

  • Enter amount of company X stocks you want to mint.
  • Pay for the stocks with USDC.
  • Tajiri places a buy order with a licensed stock broker.
  • The stock broker issues Tajiri with the actual stocks from company X.
  • Tajiri mints virtual company X stocks on the blockchain and transfers them to your wallet address.
Start minting

Option 2

Buying already minted stocks

  • Go to Tajiri peer to peer market place
  • Look for users selling their minted stocks that you are interested in.
  • Pick the right offer and purchase the stocks with USDC.
  • The minted stocks are transferred to your wallet address.
